1. How We Collect Information

1.1 Information you provide

We may collect information you provide directly, such as feedback messages, support requests, subscription-related details, and content you choose to store in the app.

1.2 Information generated while using the service

We may collect technical and usage information necessary to operate and improve the app, such as app version, device model, operating system version, language, time zone, network status, crash logs, and basic diagnostic data.

Where required by applicable law, we will obtain your consent before collecting specific categories of personal information.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide core app functions and customer support.
  • To process subscriptions and related service operations.
  • To maintain service stability, security, and fraud prevention.
  • To analyze performance and improve features and user experience.
  • To communicate important notices, including policy or service updates.

3. How We Share / Transfer / Disclose Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in limited cases, such as:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • With service providers working on our behalf under confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • During a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, with proper safeguards.

4. Information Storage & Security

  • We store information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.
  • We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information.
  • No system is absolutely secure, but we continuously improve our protections.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.

You can contact us to submit a request, and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe under applicable law.

6. Protection of Minors

We attach great importance to the protection of minors. If you are under the age required by your jurisdiction, please use the service under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published on this page with the revised “Last updated” date.
